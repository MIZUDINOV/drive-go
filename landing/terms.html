Service description

Drive GO provides tools for browsing, searching, uploading, managing, restoring, trashing, and sharing Google Drive content from the Chrome side panel through official Google APIs. The extension may also support user-initiated actions such as saving screenshots, selected text, or images to Google Drive. App settings and transfer data are generally stored on the user's own device, while network requests go to Google services only when needed for the selected feature.
