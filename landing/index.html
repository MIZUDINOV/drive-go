Security and privacy

Sensitive data is handled with minimal access

Drive GO uses official Google APIs for sign-in and Google Drive actions. Settings, transfer state, and local extension data stay in your browser on your device. Data is sent to Google only when a feature needs authentication or a Google Drive action you requested.

Local by default Your preferences, transfer state, and temporary app data stay in your browser storage on your device.
Protected in transit Data sent between the extension and Google services is transmitted over HTTPS and TLS-protected connections.
No third-party tracking No advertising trackers, no selling of user data, and no outside analytics services that are not needed for the extension to work.
User-controlled access Google access can be revoked at any time, and local extension data can be removed by clearing extension storage or uninstalling Drive GO.

Full details are available in the Privacy Policy.

FAQ

Quick answers

Is Drive GO a desktop app?

No. Drive GO is a Chrome extension.

Can I upload files with drag and drop?

Yes. Drop files into the side panel to upload them to the currently open Google Drive folder.

Do I need to open a separate Google Drive tab to upload?

Usually not. Core upload and transfer controls are available directly in the side panel.
